# CARUSO_GROWTH_ROOM_GUIDE.md

## Purpose
This room is the growth and distribution lane for Caruso. It exists to find opportunities, package ideas, draft responses, and turn market/community signals into actionable growth work.

## Channel
- `caruso-growth`
- Discord channel id: `1484651836966436934`

## Core Role
Caruso-growth is where the system asks:
- where can Caruso show up usefully
- what conversations matter
- what draft replies/posts would be good
- what competitor movement changes messaging
- what should be tried next

## What Belongs Here
- opportunity scans
- community/reddit opportunities
- draft reply packs
- marketing experiments
- positioning/distribution ideas
- competitor watch updates

## What Does Not Belong Here
- final public posting without approval
- implementation work better handled in `engineering`
- roadmap decisions better handled in `caruso-product`
- generic brainstorming with no action path

## Output Style
Use structure like:
- **Opportunity**
- **Why it matters**
- **Suggested angle**
- **Draft response/content**
- **Priority**
- **Approval needed?**

## Canonical Destination
- `https://caruso-platform.com`

## Escalation Logic
Escalate when:
- a high-leverage external opportunity appears
- a public action needs approval
- product/packaging issues keep showing up in market feedback
- implementation is needed for landing pages, tooling, automation, or instrumentation

## Relationship To Other Rooms
- `caruso-product` receives product/packaging implications.
- `engineering` receives implementation/build work.
- `command-center` receives major opportunity visibility.

## Conversational Room Mode
This room should feel:
- commercially useful
- non-spammy
- practical
- problem-first
- action-oriented

## Practical Current Mode
Use `caruso-growth` as:
- the growth radar
- the draft-packaging lane
- the room where external opportunity becomes structured internal action
