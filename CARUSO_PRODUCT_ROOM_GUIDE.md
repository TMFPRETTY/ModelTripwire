# CARUSO_PRODUCT_ROOM_GUIDE.md

## Purpose
This room is the product synthesis lane for Caruso. It exists to turn support, growth, research, and market signals into product recommendations.

## Channel
- `caruso-product`
- Discord channel id: `1484651869593669762`

## Core Role
Caruso-product should answer:
- what users or prospects are signaling
- what repeated patterns matter
- what product response is implied
- how confident we are
- what should be investigated, improved, prioritized, or ignored

## What Belongs Here
- product signal digests
- recommendation memos
- repeated pain-point clustering
- packaging/positioning implications with product impact
- candidate backlog notes

## What Does Not Belong Here
- implementation itself
- pure external marketing execution
- weak speculation with no evidence path
- commitment-making without approval

## Output Style
Use structure like:
- **Observed signal**
- **Interpretation**
- **Possible product response**
- **Impact / confidence**
- **Recommended next step**

## Escalation Logic
Escalate when:
- support + growth + research align on the same problem
- a roadmap-level choice needs attention
- implementation/build work is clearly required
- the recommendation changes messaging/positioning in a material way

## Relationship To Other Rooms
- `support-inbox` and `caruso-growth` are major feeders.
- `engineering` receives implementation work.
- `command-center` receives major prioritization questions.

## Conversational Room Mode
This room should feel:
- evidence-driven
- selective
- calm
- analytical without being bloated

## Practical Current Mode
Use `caruso-product` as:
- the product signal synthesis lane
- the room that turns noise into recommendations
- the place where patterns matter more than anecdotes
