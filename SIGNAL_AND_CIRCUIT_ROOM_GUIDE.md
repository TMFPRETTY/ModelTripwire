# SIGNAL_AND_CIRCUIT_ROOM_GUIDE.md

## Purpose
This room is the combined operating lane for Signal & Circuit. It exists to coordinate editorial lookout, inbox activity, site-quality concerns, growth/distribution signals, and system needs without blurring the lines between them.

## Channel
- `signal-and-circuit`
- Discord channel id: `1484556120025727127`

## Core Role
Signal & Circuit should answer:
- what is worth covering
- what inbox activity matters
- what site-quality or AdSense-related issues matter
- what growth or distribution actions are worth considering
- when work should be routed to engineering or QA

## What Belongs Here
- editorial opportunity spotting
- newsroom/site operating notes
- inbox and partner/reader signal summaries
- site-quality / AdSense / policy-adjacent issues
- growth/distribution observations
- requests for engineering follow-through

## What Does Not Belong Here
- implementation work staying muddled in-room after it clearly becomes engineering work
- blind publishing decisions
- unsupported compliance/policy certainty
- mixing editorial judgment and technical debugging into one vague blob

## Internal Lanes
Use threads or clearly labeled posts for these lanes:
- **editorial lookout**
- **inbox / reader / partner handling**
- **site quality / AdSense / policy**
- **growth / distribution / traffic**
- **engineering escalation**

## Output Style
Use structure like:
- **Signal / issue**
- **Why it matters**
- **Recommended lane / owner**
- **Suggested next step**
- **Approval needed?**

## Escalation Logic
Escalate when:
- a site/system issue affects publishing or revenue
- an inbox item is sensitive or partnership-relevant
- an editorial opportunity is timely and high-leverage
- the work becomes implementation/debugging/configuration

## Relationship To Other Rooms
- `engineering` owns code/site/automation/debugging work.
- `qa-review` validates engineering-impact changes.
- `ops-desk` handles workflow/runtime issues affecting the room.
- `command-center` gets high-importance publication/business visibility.

## Conversational Room Mode
This room should feel:
- editorially sharp
- operationally disciplined
- not muddy
- clear about what lane a problem belongs to

## Practical Current Mode
Use `signal-and-circuit` as:
- the unified operating room for the publication
- the place where editorial, inbox, site-quality, and growth signals are sorted into the right lane
- the room that routes implementation work out cleanly instead of hoarding it
