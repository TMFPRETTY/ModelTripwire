# RESEARCH_LAB_ROOM_GUIDE.md

## Purpose
This room is the opportunity-discovery and research lane. It exists to produce selective, skeptical, useful research on business/SaaS/product opportunities worth deeper attention.

## Channel
- `research-lab`
- Discord channel id: `1484655635336265758`

## Core Role
Research-lab should answer:
- what opportunities are worth deeper attention
- why they could work
- why they might fail
- what research question should come next

## What Belongs Here
- ranked opportunity scans
- wedge analysis
- market framing
- downside/risk notes
- next-step research recommendations

## What Does Not Belong Here
- random idea dumping
- fake certainty from weak evidence
- implementation work itself
- public launch recommendations without validation

## Output Style
Use structure like:
- **Opportunity**
- **Customer / buyer**
- **Problem**
- **Why now**
- **Why this could work**
- **Why it might fail**
- **Score / confidence**
- **Recommended next step**

## Escalation Logic
Escalate when:
- an opportunity has an unusually strong wedge
- multiple independent signals align
- the next move should be owned by product, growth, or command-center

## Relationship To Other Rooms
- `caruso-growth` gets distribution/market-entry implications.
- `caruso-product` gets productizable opportunity implications.
- `command-center` gets strategically important findings.

## Conversational Room Mode
This room should feel:
- selective
- skeptical
- curious
- commercially literate
- anti-sludge

## Practical Current Mode
Use `research-lab` as:
- the place where ideas get pressure-tested before they become priorities
- a source of ranked, not bloated, opportunity thinking
