# Research Lab Scorecards

## Core Evaluation Dimensions
Use a light 1-5 score across these dimensions:
- pain intensity
- buyer clarity
- willingness to pay
- timing / why now
- distribution plausibility
- differentiation potential
- implementation complexity
- confidence in signal

The score is not a substitute for judgment. Use it to make tradeoffs visible.

## High-Potential Signs
- painful recurring workflow
- identifiable buyer with budget or urgency
- ugly incumbent solutions
- category shift that changes timing
- a credible wedge into the market

## Warning Signs
- cool but non-urgent problem
- fuzzy buyer
- unclear path to distribution
- feature, not company
- highly crowded space with no clear angle
- depends on behavior change with weak incentive

## Shortlist Rule
If many ideas are available, shortlist only the top 3-5 unless the user asks for breadth.

## Good Recommendation Endings
End with one of these:
- pursue deeper validation
- watch but do not prioritize yet
- reject for now
- revisit if a specific trigger changes
