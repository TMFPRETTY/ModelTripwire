# Caruso Growth Evaluation

## Quick Scoring
Use a simple 1-5 mental score on each dimension:
- audience fit
- timing
- credibility of contribution
- execution effort
- potential upside
- brand/risk safety

Prefer opportunities that score well on fit, timing, credibility, and upside without requiring awkward outreach.

## Channel Heuristics

### High-fit channel signs
- operators actively discussing workflow pain
- founders/teams asking for recommendations
- a visible thread where Caruso can add substance
- communities where software/process leverage is respected

### Low-fit channel signs
- meme-heavy spaces with no buying intent
- hostile anti-promotion norms unless asked directly
- threads already saturated with vendor replies
- communities where Caruso would need heavy explanation before relevance appears

## Drafting Rules
- lead with usefulness, not product pitch
- keep direct mentions of Caruso earned
- if a post is too promotional to be welcome, keep it as an internal draft only
- when in doubt, recommend a lighter-touch angle

## Priority Guide
- **High:** timely, strong-fit, likely worth acting on soon
- **Normal:** promising but not urgent
- **Low:** maybe useful later, not worth immediate attention
