# Signal and Circuit AdSense Audit Checklist

## Page Types To Inspect
- homepage
- article detail pages
- category pages
- tag pages
- archives
- author pages if present
- search results pages if public
- About / Contact / Privacy / Terms pages

## High-Priority Checks
### Originality / value
- Does the page add real commentary, curation, insight, or reporting?
- Would the page still be useful if the reader had already seen the source material elsewhere?

### Thinness
- Is there enough substance to justify the page existing?
- Is the page mostly boilerplate, lists, embeds, or recycled context?

### Duplication
- Is similar copy repeated across many pages?
- Are there multiple pages targeting slight keyword variations with little real difference?

### UX / trust
- Are navigation and links working?
- Can a new user understand the site quickly?
- Are About, Contact, and Privacy easy to find?

### Search spam risk
- Does any page feel like it exists mainly to capture keywords?
- Are titles/promises stronger than the actual content delivered?

## Remediation Priorities
1. remove or improve obvious thin/duplicate pages
2. strengthen originality on article templates
3. fix site navigation/trust pages
4. reduce weak archive/tag surface area if needed
5. resubmit only after the site feels substantively better
