# Signal and Circuit Growth Playbook

## Good Traffic Opportunities
Look for:
- genuinely interesting breaking news
- stories with strong fandom/community energy
- industry shifts with broader implications
- opinion/analysis angles that add meaning beyond a headline recap
- recurring beats that can become habit-forming for readers

## Packaging Questions
Ask:
- Why would a reader click this now?
- Why would they stay?
- Why would they share it?
- What related piece should they read next?

## Distribution Heuristics
Prioritize channels where:
- gaming readers already gather
- the story has natural relevance
- the post can contribute something, not just self-promote
- the audience can become repeat readers, not just one-off visitors

## Avoid
- empty clickbait
- keyword-stuffed headlines
- posting everywhere with the same copy
- chasing every minor patch note or rumor
- forcing stories that do not have real reader pull
